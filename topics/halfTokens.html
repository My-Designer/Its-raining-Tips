This is one of three settings in which you choose a number.

That number cannot be lower than 20.

The proposed number is 200. You can choose another number if you like.

More tokens cause more rain, and the amount of rain is bounded. This option determines how fast high tips approach that boundary.

You define how many tokens are needed to get half of the largest possible rain. Up to that amount of tokens, visitors easily see that more tokens cause more rain. Above that amount of tokens, even higher tips cause just a little bit more rain. The nearer they get to the highest possible rain, the harder it becomes to get more rain.

Tokens needed to get half of …

… that largest rain, e. g. 200.

This is one of three settings in which you choose a number.

That number cannot be lower than 20.

The proposed number is 200. You can choose another number if you like.

More tokens cause more rain, up to a limit defined by the previous setting. This setting determines how fast higher tips approach that limit.

A medium sized rainfall is half of the limit, in other words: Half of the largest possible rainfall. This setting defines how many tokens are needed to get a medium sized rainfall. Less tokens cause less rain, just like a visitor would expect. More tokens cause slightly more rain, up to the limit defined in the previous setting.

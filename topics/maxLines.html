Maximum number of lines in …

… largest possible rain, e.g. 50.

This is one of three settings in which you choose a number.

That number cannot be lower than 5 nor higher than 70.

The proposed number is 50. You can choose another number if you like.

When a single tip comes with many tokens, this app displays rain. More tokens make more rain, and the amount of that rain is bounded. This option defines the largest possible rain, measured in chat lines.
